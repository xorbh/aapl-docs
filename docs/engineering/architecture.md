# Manufacturing System Architecture

This page outlines the structure and organization of our manufacturing documentation and quality management system.

## Documentation Structure

### Quality Standards & Compliance
- ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System
- ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management
- ISO 45001:2018 Occupational Health & Safety
- AS9100 Aerospace (where applicable)

### Equipment & Operations Manuals
- CNC Machining Centers
- Automated Turning Centers
- Precision Grinding Systems
- Welding & Assembly Stations
- Material Handling Equipment

### Work Instructions & Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)
- Production process workflows
- Equipment operation procedures
- Quality inspection checklists
- Assembly and testing protocols
- Maintenance schedules

### Key Organizational Areas

**Manufacturing:**
- Production process documentation
- Equipment maintenance procedures
- Production scheduling guidelines
- Capacity planning

**Quality:**
- Quality standards and acceptance criteria
- Inspection procedures
- Test specifications
- Defect classification

**Operations:**
- Onboarding procedures
- Production scheduling
- Shift checklists
- Work order management

**Safety:**
- Workplace safety protocols
- Emergency procedures
- Personal protective equipment (PPE) requirements
- Hazard communications

**Supply Chain:**
- Procurement standards
- Supplier qualification
- Material specifications
- Inventory management

## System Diagram

Manufacturing documentation flows from corporate standards down through equipment manuals to specific work instructions for each operator.

```
Corporate Standards (ISO, AS9100)
    ↓
Equipment Classification & Manuals
    ↓
Work Instructions (SOPs)
    ↓
Operator Execution & Logging
    ↓
Quality Verification & Records
```
